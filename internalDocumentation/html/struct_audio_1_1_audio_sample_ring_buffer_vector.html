Detailed Description

A AudioSampleRingBufferVector object is a list of ring buffers for audio samples with selection by index and left or right rotation on the individual buffers.

Makes a list of sample ring buffers for channelCount channels with arbitrary length; depending on hasTwoRingBuffersPerChannel either one or two ring buffers are provided per channel; initial sample ring buffer length is sampleRingBufferLength
